Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie

Scientist and 3rd President of Indonesia

Listed below is the timeline of BJ Habibie's life:

  • 1936 - Born in Gorontalo, Sulawesi island, Indonesia.
  • 1954 - Studied engineering in Bandung Institute of Technology.
  • 1960 - Got engineering diploma on airplane construction from RWTH Aachen, Germany.
  • 1965 - Got docorate degree with summa cum laude from RWTH Aachen.
  • 1973 - Went back to Indonesia because of request from President Suharto to work for Indonesia government.
  • 1978 - Got elected as Minister of Research and Technology.
  • 1998 - Got elected as President of Indonesia.

"I think he is a statesman who should be used as an example and role model in life."

-- Indonesian President Joko Widodo
